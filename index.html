Roy Xie

Duke University ruoyu.xie@duke.edu

I am a second-year Computer Science PhD student at Duke University, advised by Bhuwan Dhingra. I am a member of DukeNLP. My research focuses on language model efficiency. My current work investigates efficient inference methods for complex reasoning and long sequences.

My research is supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. Before Duke, I worked with Antonios Anastasopoulos on low-resource NLP.
