Ruoyu Xie

Ruoyu Xie is a senior Computer Science student at George Mason University. He is interested in various aspects of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. He has been working with Dr. Antonios Anastasopoulos at GMNLP lab for past one and a half years, focusing on creating efficient and accessible language technologies in low-resource domains.

Previously, he spent some time working in industry and solving real-word machine learning challenges. During the summer of 2022, he interned at IQVIA as a Machine Learning Engineer, where he worked with Dr. Chang Liu on building regression models for a recommendation system and models interpretability. Prior to that, he interned at AWS, working with Dr. Aziz Nazha on healthcare image classification problems. He also spent about a year interning as a Software Engineer at Precisely where he worked on front-end and back-end development.

Research interests

  • Multilingual & Low-resource Language Processing
  • Language Representations
  • Machine Learning for NLP
